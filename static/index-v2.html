Why This Is Different

Built by a Psychology Master's graduate

Not another recommendation engine. A behavioral intelligence system.

Most food AI models treat food as data — collaborative filtering, content-based matching, price optimization. FoodDNA models food as psychology: identity, habit, emotion, culture, social dynamics, and life context.

The difference? A traditional system says "users like you ordered biryani." FoodDNA says "You have a Friday celebration habit loop — biryani is the routine that satisfies your weekend reward-seeking. If unavailable, you want something equally indulgent, not a salad."

Habit Loop

Cue, Routine, Reward. We detect your food habits from ordering patterns.

Stages of Change

Never push health on someone not ready. We detect your readiness stage.

Self-Determination

Autonomy, not commands. Nudge, not force. You choose, we suggest.

Identity Theory

Dietary identity is non-negotiable. A Jain stays Jain. Always.

Behavioral Economics

Show savings, not discounts. Frame value, not cheap. Anchor with original price.

Cultural Psychology

Friday biryani. Rain pakora. Festival sweets. Indian food is identity.
